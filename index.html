Instructions

  • Your goal is to find as many Wanted Characters as possible before the time runs out.
  • You can see your time left on the left side of your screen.
  • The current Wanted Character will be displayed on the left side of your screen,
    underneath your time left.
  • Every Wanted Character you find will get an additional two seconds to the time left
    and one point to your score.
  • Every time you click without it being on the Wanted Character, you will lose one second.
  • When the time runs out, the game will end.
